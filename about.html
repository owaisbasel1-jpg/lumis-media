About Lumis Media

A media studio built by people obsessed with good stories.

We started Lumis Media to close the gap between brand strategy and the kind of content people actually want to watch, read and share.

Our mission

Make every brand worth paying attention to.

Lumis Media was founded on a simple idea: great strategy means nothing if the content it produces gets scrolled past. We bring brand thinking and craft production together under one roof.

Today we work with founders, marketing teams and media brands across Europe — helping them plan, produce and distribute content that actually earns attention.

What we believe

The principles behind every project.

Craft over volume

We'd rather ship ten great pieces than a hundred forgettable ones.

Data-informed, not data-ruled

Numbers guide decisions; they don't replace taste and judgement.

Radically transparent

Clear scopes, honest timelines, and no surprise invoices.

Built for every audience

Accessible, multi-language work by default — not an afterthought.
